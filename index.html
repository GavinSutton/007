How To Play

Press to shoot enemy

Press to block the enemy's shot

Press to reload ammunition

Each player starts with three heart points. To win, make your opponent lose all three of their heart points by shooting them. You can only shoot if you have ammmo, and you can only hit an enemy when they are reloading or shooting at you.
